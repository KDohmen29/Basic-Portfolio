About Me

I come from a small town in Minnesota, or what some people would call Minnesnowta! I grew up in a country house, in the woods. When I was younger, I was always interested in building things and finding out how random things work, especially electronics. I never really got into depth about learning how to code, but almost everyone I know tells me that they think I will be really good at it. I always had it in my mind that I would eventually try to learn and see how I like it. Six years after moving out to California, I finally decided to take the leap and sign up for school!
